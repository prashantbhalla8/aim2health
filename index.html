AIM-2-HEALTH

Aim2Health is an initiative to minimise the problems faced by a common man in getting treated by the doctor in a hospital. In this we aim to build a software which will allow the patient to get the doctor’s appointment beforehand and could skip the long queues in which he/she has to wait. The estimated time of the appointment would be given from the doctor’s end and there would be attendance of the patients when they present themselves, it would save the time of other patients too, knowing the number of patients present before them so that they could plan their visit accordingly. It would also maintain the decorum of the hospital when there will be less crowding and also the doctors would be able to work efficiently with less chaos. Also, there is no such database where the patient’s previous record and other health conditions are stored, if such database is created it would be really easy and efficient for the patients and doctors. The activities are spread across the government hospitals in Chandigarh and nearby areas, where the crowding is extremely large to handle and the patients face a lot of problems.
